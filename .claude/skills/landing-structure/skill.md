## A/B Test Hypotheses

**Hypothesis 1: Headline Test**
- **Control**: "Turn Data Into Decisions in 5 Minutes"
- **Variant**: "Stop Wasting 10 Hours/Week on Reports"
- **Metric**: CTR on hero CTA
- **Expected Impact**: Pain-first may convert better for frustrated users

**Hypothesis 2: CTA Text**
- **Control**: "Start Free 14-Day Trial"
- **Variant**: "Get My Free Trial"
- **Metric**: Conversion rate
- **Expected Impact**: First-person may increase ownership

**Hypothesis 3: Social Proof Placement**
- **Control**: Testimonials in Section 6
- **Variant**: Move one testimonial to Hero section
- **Metric**: Scroll depth + conversion rate
- **Expected Impact**: Early social proof may boost trust faster
